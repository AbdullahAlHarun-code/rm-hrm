About us

RM Human Resource Limited is an Irish based company. It is one of Ireland’s leading recruitment agency with over 3 years’ experience. RM Human Resource Limited is a trusted agency that is specialized in offering recruitment solutions to companies and organisations, also, providing work permit consultation and aims to accommodate clients with long-term employment, world-wide.

Our story

Established in 2016, RM Human Resource Limited established itself for jobs across Ireland initially focused in vast meat factories. The company has now expanded to provide consultations, work permit and jobs globally, around the world.

RM Human Resource Limited reputation is based on forming long-term partnerships with the companies they aim to work with and their clients, providing them with innovative and cost effective solutions.
This understanding and knowledge is due to the experience and quality of the team. Their highly motivated consultants are guided with a strong management panel, focused and committed to what they aim for.
RM Human Resource Limited success is driven by the philosophy to provide high quality, professional work and service to the clients. The mission – to match the right job to the right candidate!
